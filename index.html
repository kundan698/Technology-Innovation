As the number of applications a journalist must handle grows, at almost the same rate as the choice for content, easier ways to manage metadata creation must be found, even when development resources are limited. This presentation covers some of TV 2 Denmark's thinking in their drive to enrich content with metadata in the newsroom and make content more "findable" in production.
